Hostel Overview

Welcome to the hostels at Don Bosco College, Yelagiri Hills! Our hostels are designed to provide a safe, comfortable, and conducive environment for students to thrive academically and personally. With modern amenities and a supportive community, we aim to enhance your college experience.

Our facilities include Wi-Fi, 24/7 security, recreational areas, and well-maintained dining services. The hostels are managed by dedicated personnel to ensure a high standard of living.
