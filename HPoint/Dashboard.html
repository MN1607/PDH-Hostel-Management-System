About PDH Hostel

PDH Hostel, nestled in the scenic Yelagiri Hills, offers a peaceful and conducive environment for students of Don Bosco College. Our hostel provides a safe, comfortable living space to support your academic journey amidst nature.

As a co-educational facility, we foster a holistic experience with well-maintained dormitories, recreational areas, and 24/7 security to ensure your well-being.

We prioritize community and growth, creating a supportive atmosphere for all residents.

About Don Bosco College

Established in 1983, Don Bosco College, Yelagiri Hills, is a premier co-educational institution affiliated with Thiruvalluvar University. Located in the serene hills of Tamil Nadu, the college offers a perfect blend of academic excellence and holistic development.

We provide a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including B.Sc., B.Com, BCA, and M.A., with state-of-the-art facilities like modern labs, a well-stocked library, and sports amenities.

Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, skills, and values, fostering leadership and innovation in a nurturing environment.
